THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO PARIDHI

My top 3 movies of all-time.

IT

A group of bullied kids band together to destroy a shape-shifting monster, which disguises itself as a clown and preys on the children of Derry, their small Maine town.

Interstellar

Earth's last chance to find a habitable planet before a lack of resources causes the human race to go extinct.

Shutter Island

A U.S. Marshal who goes to an isolated hospital for the criminally insane to investigate the disappearance of a patient who is a multiple murderer.
